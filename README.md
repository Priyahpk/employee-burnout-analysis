# employee-burnout-analysis

In high-performance organizations,
employee burnout and disengagement
significantly impact productivity,
innovation, and retention. This study focuses
on developing a proactive analytical
framework to identify early signs of burnout
and declining engagement among employees
at Palo Alto Networks. By constructing an
Engagement Index and a Burnout Risk
Score, this project enables early detection of
workforce challenges, allowing HR teams to
implement timely interventions. The results
highlight strong relationships between
overtime, work-life balance, and employee
satisfaction, demonstrating that
disengagement often precedes attrition
